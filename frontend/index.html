Istanbul SDI Readiness Dashboard

Evaluating the strategic capacity of the Turkish SDI (TUCBS) for 1:10,000 topographic resilience.

Research Objectives

  • Data Accessibility: Moving from "Known" official data to "Usable" local hosting.
  • Preparedness: Identifying technical attributes (load capacity, clearance) for military mobilization[cite: 44, 98].
  • Seismic Resilience: Mapping critical nodes in the Bosphorus corridor[cite: 79, 97].

This project uses the Backx (2003) model to assess the reliability of spatial data during emergency scenarios[cite: 61].
